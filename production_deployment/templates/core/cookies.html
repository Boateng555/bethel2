What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files that are placed on your device when you visit our website. They help us provide you with a better experience and understand how you use our site.

How We Use Cookies

We use cookies for the following purposes:

  • Essential Cookies: Required for basic site functionality and security
  • Authentication Cookies: Remember your login status and preferences
  • Analytics Cookies: Help us understand how visitors use our site
  • Functional Cookies: Remember your preferences and settings
  • Marketing Cookies: Help us provide relevant content and advertisements

Types of Cookies We Use

Session Cookies

These cookies are temporary and are deleted when you close your browser. They help maintain your session and security.

Persistent Cookies

These cookies remain on your device for a set period and help us remember your preferences and improve your experience.

Third-Party Cookies

We may use third-party services (like Google Analytics) that place their own cookies. These services have their own privacy policies.

Managing Cookies

You can control and manage cookies in several ways:

  • Browser settings: Most browsers allow you to block or delete cookies
  • Opt-out tools: Use browser extensions or tools to manage cookies
  • Contact us: Request specific cookie management assistance

Cookie Preferences

When you first visit our site, you'll see a cookie consent banner. You can change your preferences at any time by contacting us or adjusting your browser settings.

Updates to This Policy

We may update this Cookie Policy from time to time. Please check back regularly for the latest information.

Contact Us
