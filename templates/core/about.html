About Us

Our History

Founded in 2005, Bethel Prayer Ministry International began as a small, Spirit-filled prayer group in response to a vision God gave to Rev. Benjamin Mensah. With a handful of believers, Rev. Mensah started weekly gatherings focused on fervent prayer, worship, and the study of God’s Word. The group quickly grew as people experienced healing, deliverance, and transformation through the power of prayer.

Over the years, Bethel Prayer Ministry International has blossomed into a dynamic, multi-cultural church family with branches in the United Kingdom, United States, and other nations. The ministry is known for its passionate worship, powerful intercession, and a deep commitment to seeing lives changed by the love of Jesus Christ.

Our Founder

Rev. Benjamin Mensah is a visionary leader, teacher, and intercessor. He is deeply committed to raising up a generation of believers who are rooted in prayer and equipped to impact their world. Rev. Mensah’s ministry is marked by humility, compassion, and a relentless pursuit of God’s presence. He is supported by a dedicated team of pastors, elders, and ministry leaders who share his heart for revival and community transformation.

What We Do

Bethel Prayer Ministry International is more than a church—it’s a movement. Our ministry is built on four pillars:

  • Prayer: We believe prayer is the engine of spiritual growth and breakthrough. We host daily and weekly prayer meetings, all-night vigils, and special prayer conferences that draw people from across the globe.
  • Worship: Our worship services are vibrant and Spirit-led, creating an atmosphere where people can encounter God, receive healing, and be refreshed.
  • Teaching & Discipleship: We offer Bible studies, discipleship classes, and leadership training to help believers grow in faith and maturity. Our teaching is rooted in Scripture and relevant to everyday life.
  • Outreach & Missions: We are passionate about reaching the lost and serving our communities. Our outreach programs include food distribution, support for the homeless, hospital and prison ministry, youth empowerment, and evangelistic crusades.

Our Global Impact

Bethel Prayer Ministry International has touched thousands of lives through its local and international branches. Our annual conventions and prayer summits bring together believers from different nations to seek God’s face and receive fresh vision for the year ahead. Through our online ministry, we reach people in Africa, Europe, North America, and beyond—offering hope, encouragement, and practical support.

Our Mission

To spread the gospel of Jesus Christ and make disciples of all nations through prayer, teaching, and community service.

Our Vision

To be a global spiritual center where lives are transformed through the power of prayer and the Word of God.

Core Values

  • Prayer: We believe in the transformative power of prayer.
  • Biblical Teaching: We are committed to sound biblical doctrine.
  • Service: We serve God by serving others.
  • Community: We foster a loving and supportive community.
  • Integrity: We strive to live out our faith with honesty and transparency.
  • Compassion: We reach out to the hurting and marginalized with the love of Christ.

Join Us

Whether you are seeking a church home, need prayer, or want to grow in your faith, Bethel Prayer Ministry International welcomes you. We are a family of believers from all backgrounds, united by our love for God and our desire to make a difference in the world. Come and experience the power of prayer, the warmth of community, and the life-changing message of Jesus Christ.
