Red5

How to get this URL:

  • Red5 Pro (your server): Open your Red5 embeddable player in a browser (e.g. https://your-red5-server.com/truetime-multiview/?host=your-red5-server.com&StreamName=yourstream). Copy the full address from the browser bar and paste it here.
  • Red5 Cloud: In your Red5 Cloud dashboard, open your deployment → Pub/Sub Details. Copy the Embedded Player iframe code, then copy only the src="..." URL from inside the iframe and paste that URL here.
  • Any page URL that shows your Red5 stream in a browser can be used, as long as it works inside an iframe.
